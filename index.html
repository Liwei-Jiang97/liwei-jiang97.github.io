About Me

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the H. Milton Stewart School of Industrial and Systems Engineering (ISyE) at Georgia Institute of Technology, where I work with Professor Ashwin Pananjady and Professor Katya Scheinberg . I am interested in problems that lie at the intersection of optimization, geometry, statistics, and data science.

In 2024, I received my Ph.D. from the School of Operations Research and Information Engineering at Cornell University, where I was very fortunate to be advised by Professor Damek Davis. Prior to graduate school, I obtained my B.S. from the Department of Mathematics at Nanjing University in 2019. In 2018, I spent one year as a visiting student in the Department of Mathematics at the University of Wisconsin-Madison.

Outside of research, I am an avid climber. I have just sent Dragon Turns (V9) at Gunks!
